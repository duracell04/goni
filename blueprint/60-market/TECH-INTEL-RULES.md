# Technology Intel Rules

This lane exists to improve Goni's technical decisions through external comparison.

## Scope
- competitors
- suppliers
- partners
- other ecosystem players

## What to capture
- product surface and deployment topology
- architecture and runtime model
- trust model (permissions, mediation, auditability)
- extension/plugin/marketplace risks
- operational traits (offline mode, update model, observability)
- direct implications for Goni specs

## What not to capture
- concrete prices
- pricing plan details
- speculative monetization claims

## Card format (recommended)
1. What it is
2. Technology profile
3. Trust and governance profile
4. Integration relevance for Goni
5. Risks and blind spots
6. Sources with snapshot date
